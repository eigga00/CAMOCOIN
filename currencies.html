About Us

Bitcoin- Bitcoin is the first decentralized cryptocurrency that influenced proceeding cryptocurrencies that were created. In 2009 a paper called Bitcoin: A Peer-to-Peer Electronic Cash System was published on the internet by the pseudonym Satoshi Nakamoto which explained Bitcoin and how it works. Bitcoin runs on a system called blockchain which gives it its unique properties. Bitcoin has a limit of twenty one million coins that can be created and has a transaction confirmation time of around ten minutes. Blocks (which contains around 12.5 Bitcoins) are mined about every ten minutes.

Ethereum (ETH)- Launched in 2015, Ethereum is a public blockchain network. Ethereum focuses on running the code of any decentralized program. Ether, the currency of Ethereum, acts as a type of fuel for the or motivation for the applications on the network. Miners to work to earn Ether which is not only a tradable cryptocurrency but is also used to pay for services and transactions on the server. There is another kind of payment called gas, every smart contract requires some gsa ot sent along with it to give motive for miners to add it to the block. There is no limit of the amount of ether.
Ripple Coin (XRP)-Ripple Coin was co-founded in 2012 by Chris Larsen and jed McCaleb. Unlike Bitcoin and Ethereum, Ripple Coin does not have a blockchain system. Instead, they have created the Ripple Protocol Consensus Algorithm (RPCA) where every node must be in agreement with the others. In addition, Ripple Coins are pre-mined with a maximum supply of 100 billion Ripple Coins, meaning that "mining" Ripple Coin does not generate new coins.
Cardano (ADA)-Cardano was launched in 2015 by Charles Hoskinson, a former Ethereum Co-Founder. Similar to Bitcoin and Ethereum, Cardano runs on a decentralized application and has a blockchain system. However, Cardano runs on a technological platform that will be capable of running financial applications currently used everyday by individuals, companies, and governments. Cardano has a circulating supply of 26 billion ADA and a maximum supply of 45 billion ADA.
