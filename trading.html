Buying Cryptocurrency

1. Choose an exchange

These are some trusted exchanges, or places where you can trade cryptocurrency:

ChangeNow

Kraken

2. Create an account on your chosen exchange.

You will need government issued identitfication to create an account

3. Connect your method of payment and buy cryptocurrency!
